Disposable Endoscopes Market Expands Driven by Infection Control and Technology Advancements

LONDON, Feb. 21, 2024 – The latest research from The Business Research Company highlights the significant growth in the disposable endoscopes market. The industry has experienced a remarkable upsurge in recent years, fueled by factors such as infection control, regulatory approvals, cost-efficiency, and a rising preference for minimally invasive procedures. According to market analysis, the disposable endoscopes market is expected to grow from $1.62 billion in 2023 to $1.91 billion in 2024, reflecting a notable compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 18.1%. Looking ahead, projections indicate a surge to $3.53 billion by 2028, with a CAGR of 16.6%, driven by improved global healthcare access, the trend towards outpatient procedures, and the adoption of telemedicine and remote consultations.

Key Trends and Drivers

Specialized disposable endoscopes tailored for specific medical procedures, advancements in imaging technologies, sustainability initiatives, and the integration of artificial intelligence are anticipated as major trends shaping the market. The growth is further accelerated by the increasing number of surgical operations globally, highlighting the role of endoscopic procedures in enhancing patient outcomes and reducing recovery time.

Market Players and Innovations

Leading companies in the disposable endoscopes market, including Medtronic PLC, Fujifilm Holdings Corporation, and Boston Scientific Corporation, are focusing on innovation to maintain their competitive edge. Notable advancements such as Ambu Inc.’s Ambu aScope 5 Broncho and ATL Technology’s acquisition of BioVision Technologies underscore the industry’s commitment to developing cutting-edge solutions.

Regional Insights and Segmentation

While North America dominated the market in 2023, other regions, particularly Asia-Pacific, are expected to witness rapid growth. The market is segmented by product, patient type, and end-user, catering to various medical needs across hospitals, ambulatory surgical centers, and diagnostic centers. In conclusion, the Disposable Endoscopes Industry Anticipates Rapid Growth report serves as a valuable resource for industry players to navigate the evolving landscape of disposable endoscopy. The forecasted market expansion, driven by technological advancements and healthcare trends, presents significant opportunities for growth and innovation in the disposable endoscopes sector.
